KLOUD BUGS is an exclusive AI-powered mining platform that unifies diverse mining hardware under a unified command center while preserving original manufacturer specifications.

Our platform features advanced optimization technology that maximizes mining efficiency across all hardware types, creating a powerful collective mining experience.

Our Mission: Mining for Justice

KLOUD BUGS is dedicated to supporting social justice initiatives through the power of blockchain technology. Our platform channels a portion of mining rewards toward legal accountability projects and advocacy for those affected by systemic injustice.

Named in honor of Tera Ann Harris, the TERA token represents our commitment to creating lasting positive change by combining technological innovation with social responsibility.

"Technology should be a force for positive change in society. We're mining not just for profit, but for justice."
