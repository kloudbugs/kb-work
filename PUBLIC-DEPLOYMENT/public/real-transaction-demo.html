About Bitcoin Transactions

Security

Bitcoin transactions are secured by cryptography and distributed consensus. Once confirmed, transactions cannot be reversed or altered, providing immutable proof of the transfer.

Transparency

All Bitcoin transactions are recorded on a public blockchain that anyone can verify. This transparency ensures accountability while maintaining pseudonymity.
